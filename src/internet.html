Questions Answers
Describe key events in the development of the internet from the 1980s to today? The Internet allowed information to be shared across different computers from great distances away almost instantly. The Internet became accessable to the public, and rapidly became an essential part of infastructure. Web browers in the 90's transformed our abiity to look up information and mobile access to the internet further revolutionised the use of the internet.
Define and describes the relationship between fundamental aspects of the internet such as: domains, web servers, DNS, and web browsers? Domains: Basically the URL/address of a webpage, this can be bought. Web Server: The primary function of a web server is to store, process and deliver web pages to clients DNS server: It keeps records of IP address and corresponding domain names. Web browesers: Basically google, allowing people to simply look up things and get related results spat back to them. Rather than having to remember links or have a webpage full of links to other sites
Reflect on one aspect of the development of internet technologies and how it has contributed to the world today? Social media: it has radicaly transformed our world in a very short space of time, communication between complete strangers across the globe is now possible as well as people able to quickly find themselves in groups with mutual interestes and hobbies. It has lead to the change of the political landscape and election results. It has debatably made us both more social and antisocial/disconnected from others
